How to Calculate Overtime Hours and Pay

Calculating overtime is straightforward with this tool. Enter your base hourly rate and total hours worked during the pay period. The calculator automatically determines how many hours qualify as overtime based on your threshold setting. For example, if you worked 50 hours in a week with a 40-hour threshold, you have 10 overtime hours. With a standard 1.5x overtime rate (time and a half), those overtime hours are paid at 150% of your regular hourly rate.

Time and a Half Calculator for Overtime Wages
